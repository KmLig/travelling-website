Cà Ná


Cà Na beach in Ninh Thuan is one of the most beautiful beaches in Vietnam, those who come here are mesmerized by the wonderful beauty of this place. Ca Na is a rather special name on the map of Vietnam, with the natural beauty of mountains, forests and sea combined with the beauty of Cham culture, making this place attractive to many tourists. domestically and internationally.

Vĩnh Hy bay


The white waves of the waves and the stunning beauty of the sea and sky make this place extremely wonderful. Vinh Hy Bay is known as an oasis with soft winding roads embracing nature. With the wild beauty of nature, Vinh Hy Bay is a picture of a fairyland that captivates many tourists. You cannot ignore this Ninh Thuan tourist destination in your journey.

Núi Chúa National Park


With a total natural area of over 24,300 ha, located on the boundary of 5 communes of Ninh Hai. Nui Chua National Park is really attracting tourists with outstanding values of topography, topography, climate and high biodiversity. This is also home to a typical dry forest ecosystem that is rarely seen in our country with many species of living things that are very rich and diverse in number and types.

From Wikipedia

An Hoa sheep field


An Hoa sheep field is located in An Hoa village, Xuan Hai commune. This place has a very large number of herds, from a few hundred to a thousand. When coming here, visitors can watch herds of sheep, admire the majestic nature, dine with farmers and discover techniques of raising and shearing sheep.
