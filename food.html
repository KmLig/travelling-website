Dried squid


Unlike normal dried squid, in order to have a sunny day, one must choose squid from squid that have just been brought back from the sea and are still fresh, must be washed with sea water and then dried only once in the sun. . The squid drying is also a miracle to make the outside body of the squid completely dry, but the inside of the squid is still fresh and when grilled, the squid still retains its freshness, the white meat is fragrant and flexible.

Phan Rang Chicken Rice


Phan Rang chicken rice is cooked with sticky, fragrant rice. Before cooking, the rice is washed, marinated with ginger and crushed garlic to create aroma. To have an eye-catching plate of rice, often cooks add a little turmeric powder to the rice. Chicken cut into medium sized pieces. This dish is served hot with some laksa leaves, cucumber and sweet and sour fish sauce prepared or dipped with salt and pepper. Another special kind of dipping sauce should be added is hummus, a unique sauce only found in Phan Rang. Garlic is also ginger, but we add a little alcohol and sugar to create a characteristic sweet and sour taste. Mustard rice is added to the rice to reduce the greasy taste. Phan Rang chicken rice must be eaten with laksa leaves to be "right", perhaps laksa leaves are especially suitable for chicken, so the more you eat it, the more delicious it becomes.

Grilled lamb chops - A must-try dish when coming to Ninh Thuan


Just after grilling, the meat is both hot and crispy, chewy, and retains its characteristic flavor. Grilled lamb chops dipped with chao or soy sauce to make the dish more delicious.

Grape wine


Drinking Phan Rang grape wine, you should not compare it with other grape wines, because the farmers here are unfamiliar with the concept of grape wine. And they also never thought that they were contributing to writing more history for world wine industry with hundreds of years of development. The people here make wine for the same simple reasons as their own life, their simple soul, just a gift from the countryside for guests.
